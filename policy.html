Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 06, 2025
STEP File Viewer values your privacy.

This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use our app. Spoiler: we don’t collect it. Here's what that really means.

1. No Data Collection

STEP file viewer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens locally on your device.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cloud uploads
  • No data sharing with us or others

Your files, models, and exports stay on your device — unless you shared them to others.

2. Offline Processing

All features in STEP file viewer run entirely offline. We never upload your files to any server, ensuring complete industrial design privacy.

3. Local Storage Only

Your data is only saved temporarily in memory and on your local device while using the app. We don’t have access to these files, and they’re never shared unless you do it yourself.

4. No Third-Party SDKs

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or any third-party services. No tracking. No fingerprinting. No hidden surprises.

5. Your Rights

Since we don’t collect any data, there’s nothing for you to request, delete, or revoke. Your intellectual property remains yours alone.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted by updating the effective date at the top of this policy.
